We currently offer 3 different slime textures:

Jelly

Our jelly textured slimes are made with clear glue and after it settles it resembles jelly.

Cloud

Our cloud textured slimes are made with white glue and resembles the look of ice cream.

Icee

Our icee textured slimes are made with clear glue and has a frosty look to it.

 
     

How to care for slime

Slime can result in being sticky if it is not kept at room temperature. Each slime comes with a care package that helps to restore the slime. The care package includes activator in a packet and instructions on how to use it. To see a visual representation on how to make the slime watch this video.
